# Overview

## What is Kinitro?
Kinitro is a permissionless incentivization engine for advancing intelligence. 
Users\* can create competitions with defined benchmarks and metrics, and participants (miners) can compete by submitting agents that aim to meet or surpass these standards. Validators evaluate the submitted agents, and the participants whose agents perform best in competitions earn rewards.

We envision this engine to be used to produce agents that can be deployed as its users see fit - whether it be totally offline on a personal robot at home, an on-prem cluster acting as the brain powering several robots at a factory or a swarm of delivery drones.

Kinitro relies on Bittensor at the heart of its incentivization mechanism. To learn more about Bittensor please read their documentation [here](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/).

\*During the early phases of the subnet, the "users" would be us (the subnet devs), and we will be hosting a set of our own competitions for miners to compete it and produce agents for.
